Scrambles

How to Play

Solve all five scrambles before the time runs out.

Anagram:

a p m x e l e

Answer:   

e x a m p l e

  • Earn 100 points plus the remaining time (in seconds) for every correctly solved scramble.

  • Each round presents longer words to challenge you.

  • If you're stuck, you can use a hint but forfeit your time bonus.

  • Remember, there might be multiple answers, but only one is correct.
